Work with us

Berkeley Innovation, a human-centered design club at UC Berkeley, is looking for design projects for Spring 2014. We are passionate about user research, product development, and UI/UX design, and we'd like to work on your next bite-sized design project.

In the past, we've done projects with large companies like Microsoft and Accenture, design firms like Vertigo, and startups like Endless Mobile and Bravo Your City. Teams of 3-6 designers devote 12 weeks, 2-8 hours/week, to your project, for free. In return, you (the project sponsor) meet with the team, and offer direction and feedback. This is a great chance to supercharge your project, and recruit future rockstars from a pool of creative undergrads.

If you'd like to work with us this Spring, contact our Partnership Chair (Josh) at jstroud@berkeley.edu.
