About

Who we are

We're an undergraduate human-centered design group at the UC Berkeley. Our members come from a wide range of disciplines, but share a love of design thinking and innovation.

What we do

Each semester, we work on projects that give members hands-on experience with the entire design process, from conducting user research to building and marketing prototypes. We also hold designathons.

How to join

Unfortunately, the application period for this semester is over. Follow us on Facebook or Twitter to be notified when we have an info session next semester! Or, if you're a company or organization interested in looking at a problem from our perspective, feel free to contact us.
